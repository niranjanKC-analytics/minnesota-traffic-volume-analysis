# 🚗 Minnesota Traffic Volume Analysis

**Business Intelligence Dashboard | Google Business Intelligence Certificate**

---

## 📌 Executive Summary
This project analyzes interstate traffic volume data to identify patterns across time, weather conditions, and holidays. The goal is to support transportation planning and congestion management through data-driven insights.

An interactive Tableau dashboard was developed to visualize trends, detect peak congestion periods, and understand external factors influencing traffic flow.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Transportation agencies need to understand traffic behavior to improve infrastructure planning and reduce congestion.

**Key Questions:**
- How does traffic volume vary by month and season?
- What are peak traffic hours during the day?
- How do weather conditions impact traffic?
- Which holidays experience the highest traffic?

---

### Data Source

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Traffic volume peaks during **summer months**
- **Rush hours (morning and evening)** show highest traffic levels
- Traffic is highest during **clear and cloudy weather conditions**
- Holidays such as **Labor Day and New Year’s Day** show increased traffic
- Nighttime traffic is significantly lower than daytime traffic

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Plan infrastructure improvements around **seasonal peak periods**
- Optimize traffic flow during **rush hours**
- Increase monitoring during **holiday travel periods**
- Use weather data for **traffic forecasting and safety planning**
